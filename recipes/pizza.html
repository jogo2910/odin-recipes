Pizza Recipe 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup diced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onions
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease a large baking sheet or pizza pan with cooking spray.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll the pizza dough into a large circle or oval, about 1/4 inch thick. Place the dough onto the prepared baking sheet or pizza pan.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the pizza dough, leaving about 1 inch border for the crust. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese, cheddar cheese, pepperoni, black olives, bell peppers, and red onions over the sauce.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the basil, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Sprinkle the mixture over the toppings on the pizza.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes. Slice and serve hot.
