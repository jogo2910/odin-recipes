Pad Thai recipe

Recipe for 4 servings

Ingredients :

  • 8 ounces of rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup of chopped peanuts
  • 1/2 cup of chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 cup of chopped bean sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped ginger
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Instructions:

  1. Soak the rice noodles in hot water for 10 minutes, or until they are soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the beaten eggs and scramble until cooked. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.
  3. Add the shrimp to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until they are pink and cooked through. Remove the shrimp from the pan and set aside.
  4. Add the chopped peanuts, green onions, cilantro, and bean sprouts to the pan and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together the fish sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and soy sauce to make the sauce.
  6. Add the sauce to the pan with the vegetables and stir to combine.
  7. Add the drained noodles to the pan and toss to combine with the sauce and vegetables.
  8. Return the cooked shrimp and scrambled eggs to the pan and stir to combine.
  9. Serve the Pad Thai hot, garnished with lime wedges.
