Ramen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 packs of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup sake or dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 lb. sliced pork belly or chicken breast
  • 4 boiled eggs, sliced in half
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring the broth, soy sauce, sake or dry white wine, mirin, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes to a boil over high heat. Reduce the heat and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, in a separate pan, cook the pork belly or chicken breast over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Remove from heat and slice into thin pieces.
  3. In a pot of boiling water, cook the ramen noodles for 2-3 minutes, or until soft. Drain and divide the noodles into 4 bowls.
  4. Pour the broth over the noodles in each bowl. Add the sliced pork or chicken, boiled eggs, green onions, mushrooms, bean sprouts, and cilantro to each bowl. Serve hot with lime wedges on the side.
